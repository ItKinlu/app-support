About the App

Mduyen Focus is a personal productivity and digital wellbeing app designed to help you stay focused by reducing distractions.

You can select apps or categories to limit during focus sessions, schedule restriction periods, and build better habits through intentional device usage.

How It Works

With your permission, the app uses Apple’s Screen Time APIs to apply restrictions to selected apps during focus sessions.

All controls are user-initiated and fully transparent. You can modify or remove restrictions at any time.
