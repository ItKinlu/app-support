Privacy Policy

Mduyen Focus respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

Mduyen Focus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

On-Device Data

The app stores necessary data locally on your device, such as focus sessions, user-selected apps or categories, and app settings. This data never leaves your device.

Screen Time API Usage

With your explicit permission, the app uses Apple’s Screen Time APIs (including FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity) to allow you to select apps and manage usage restrictions during focus sessions.

This information is provided directly by the system and is used only to enable the app’s core functionality. It is not collected, stored externally, or shared.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Account Information

The app does not require account registration or login.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
